Fun Facts about Chameleons

Fun Facts about Cicada!

  • The Cicada's corpse serves as an important source of nitrogen for growing trees
  • The Male Cicada has a very loud and unique mating call
  • Cicada's are not dangerous at all, they are not poisonous, they do not sting or bite and they do not carry any disease.
